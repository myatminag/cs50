sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: It iterates through the unsorted portions of a list, selecting the smallest element each time and moving it to its correct location.

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: It recursively divides the list into two repeatedly and then merges the smaller lists back into a larger one in the correct order.

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: It compares pairs of adjacent values one at a time and swaps them if they are in the incorrect order. This continues until the list is sorted.
